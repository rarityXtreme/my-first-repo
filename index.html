What is an Algorave?

An Algorave is an event where people dance to music generated from algorithms, using live coding techniques. The movement emerge in 2011 and the term was coined by live coders Nick Collins and Alex McLean while driving to a gig, inspired by a happy hardcore pirate radio station. Expect visuals and sonic chaos!
